Manavgat


Manavgat, a well-liked tourist resort in Turkey’s Antalya region, is well-known for its stunning beauty, extensive history, and intriguing culture. The Manavgat River, which flows through the community and enhances its natural beauty, is the source of the town’s name.When planning a trip to Turkey, make sure to include both well-known sites and undiscovered gems on your itinerary. Many people only visit the most well-known places and rarely travel to other areas that contribute to Turkey’s current identity. For instance, Manavgat is one of the best undiscovered jewels with a lot to offer both visitors and locals.Ancient ruins, gorgeous beaches, mountains, breathtaking natural beauty, delectable food, and more are among Manavgat top attractions. When strolling around this town, you may go trekking in the mountains, sunbathe or swim at the beaches, or check out the neighbourhood cafés. The top attractions in Manavgat for families can also be found in this town because it is so family-friendly.If you are planning a trip to Antalya, be sure to include Manavgat in your itinerary because it is only a short drive from the city. You will then be able to see some of Turkey’s most popular tourist attractions and more. The people are kind, the scenery is beautiful, and you will enjoy a peaceful time here.If you enjoy having experiences with your family and friends, there are several opportunities for you to join. Safaris, boat cruises, rafting, and other activities are available. The ones you select will be determined by what you want to accomplish and how long you intend to remain in Manavgat, Turkey.Hence, if you want to add a fantastic beach town to your Turkey itinerary, Manavgat is the place to go. Manavgat has some of the top attractions for children, families, couples, and others. Whichever group you are travelling with, there are plenty of things to do in this region to keep you coming back for more.




Manavgat Must-See Famous 7 Attractions



1. Manavgat Aspendos Side


A day trip is just what you need to get away from the city and enjoy the relaxing atmosphere of natural beauty and historic sites. Manavgat has waterfalls, lush greenery, a bustling bazaar, and much more for you to enjoy in this small town. You can take pictures with the greenery and waterfalls as your background, or you can enjoy the relaxing sounds of nature.On the other hand, Aspendos is an ancient city where you can explore the historic ruins that have been preserved for a long time now. It is one of the best attractions in Manavgat with kids because it will boost their knowledge about history. A tour guide will be present at all times to ensure that you understand the history of this place.Here you will find historical structures, Roman theatres, an amphitheater, and more. As you make your way through each site, you can ask questions about it and just bask in the beauty of these ruins. Everyone that comes here leaves with a sense of awe and information about the history of Turkey.So, if your children are curious and you love visiting natural and historical sites, then it doesn’t get better than this day trip. Your entire day will be filled with fun, excitement, and knowledge that you will not find anywhere else.


2. Paragliding From Manavgat



Are you and your children always looking for adventure and new things to try when you are on holiday? If that is the case with you, then paragliding is one of the top tourist attractions in Manavgat. It will allow you to enjoy the best views of this city as you soar through the sky.You will be flying for twenty minutes with a licensed professional so that you are completely safe during this time. The professionals will provide you with a safety briefing and gear so that you are prepared for the flight. Keep in mind that only children over the age of ten can participate in this experience.Once you start soaring in the sky, you will witness Manavgat like never before. The adrenaline boost and the views you will get are something you will remember for the rest of your life. As time passes, you will also feel a sense of calm and serenity in the sky that will recharge you.So, if you want to have an adventure with your children, then it doesn’t get better than paragliding from Manavgat. It will be an experience to remember that everyone will cherish for their entire life.


3. Demre Myra Kekova Tour From Manavgat



This tour is another day trip that will take you through the best attractions in Manavgat for families. You will take a boat cruise on a boat with a glass bottom to explore Kekova, an ancient underwater city that many people don’t know about. This ancient city sunk inside the sea, which is why you can explore a lot of its ruins today.After that, your next stop will be the city of Myra from historical times, which will boost your knowledge about the history of this place. Here, you will find tombs, an amphitheater, and much more. The tour guide will provide you with all the details you need to make this experience a memorable one.Finally, you will make your way to Demre, to Saint Nicholas Church. It used to be a church, but now it is a museum that you can visit and explore. Your kids will get to see natural beauty, religious structures, and an ancient city in one tour.So, book this tour today, and have a fantastic time with your family exploring everything that Manavgat has to offer. The tour has been designed for families to have a great time while they are here.


4. Manavgat Altinbesik Cave Tour



Manavgat has many natural wonders that you can explore whenever you are on your trip. The Altinbesik Cave is one such wonder that will leave you in awe. That is because you will get to witness an underground lake, traditional villages, canyons, and much more.It is one of the best attractions in Manavgat with kids because they will get to see the natural beauty of Manavgat, Turkey. As you enter the cave, you will find various rock formations that will stun you. These include stalagmites, stalactites, and more.On the other, the lake will provide you with a serene atmosphere that will be hard to find anywhere else. You will learn all about the history and natural beauty of this cave and its attraction as you make your way through. This will help you understand the landscape and why it is the way it is.So, if your kids love getting to know about natural wonders, then you should add this tour to the top of your list. It will be the highlight of your experience that you will never forget.


5. Green Canyon Boat Trip From Manavgat



The Green Canyon is one of the best attractions in Manavgat for couples that want to spend a relaxing day together by getting away from the city. That is because this boat trip will take you through the canyon that is surrounded by tall cliffs and nature. You can lounge on the deck and enjoy the peaceful views.Then, there will also be a lunch at the lake in a restaurant with the best views. As you indulge in the lunch, a sense of tranquility will wash over you when you look at the views as you eat. The chirping of the birds and the sound of the waves will be the perfect backdrop for you to enjoy a delicious meal.The canyon is also the fifth-largest dam in the entire country, and you will learn some interesting facts about it during the tour. So, not only will you enjoy beautiful views, but you will also learn something new. This is why it is a great trip to enjoy with your significant other.So, if you want to get away from the city and enjoy an atmosphere of relaxation, then you need to sign up for the Green Canyon boat trip. Once you do, you will wonder why you didn’t take this trip before.


6. Manavgat River Cruise



Families looking for a memorable experience can opt for the Manavgat River Cruise, as it is one of the best attractions in Manavgat for families. The scenery is majestic, and cruising through the river will allow you to take in the views that surround you. You can also spot the wildlife that lives in this region as you cruise through.For example, birds, sea turtles, fish, and more that is only unique to this region. Besides that, Manavgat has some of the most beautiful waterfalls in the country that will leave you breathless. As you witness these waterfalls and listen to the sounds, a wave of peace will wash over you.The cruise will allow you and your children to have a relaxing time on this trip. You can lounge on the deck or simply enjoy the views from wherever you are on the boat. Anyone visiting Manavgat needs to opt for this tour at least once in their life.Finally, you will also get to witness the culture of this region as you cruise. So, make sure that you sign up for this cruise as soon as possible to have the best time. There is quite nothing like having a beautiful and cultural experience in one.


7. Manavgat Jeep Safari & Rafting (Combo Tour)



Finally, if you want to have two adventures in one with your family, then you need to sign up for the Manavgat Jeep Safari and Rafting tour combination. You will hop on a jeep safari and make your way through the rugged yet beautiful Taurus Mountains. Then, you will enjoy a rafting experience that will boost your adrenaline.The rafting experience will be the highlight of the trip because you will get to raft through a wild river. The instructors will provide you with safety gear and instructions so that you can stay safe and know what to do. It is one of the top team-building activities that your family will enjoy.There will also be breaks during the experience where you can swim in the waters and reenergize your senses. The entire adventure has been designed to guarantee that you and your family have a great time. Even if you want to book this tour with your friends, then you will have a fantastic time too.So, book this tour today and have two adventures in one during your trip to Manavgat. You will leave with beautiful memories and a sense of rush and energy like never before.
